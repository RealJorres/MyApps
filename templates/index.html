About Jorres Apps

Jorres Apps started as a personal collection of small tools I built for my own everyday use. They turned out to be too useful to keep to myself, so I decided to share them with everyone.

What this is

Over 130 free tools and mini-games across ten categories — calculators, converters, formatters, PDF utilities, planners, and classic games. Everything runs in your browser, ready the moment the page loads. No install, no setup.

What we advertise is what you get

  • Free. Every tool, in full. No paywalls, no premium tiers, no upsells.
  • No sign-up. No accounts, no logins, no email walls. Open a tool and use it.
  • No tracking. No analytics, no ads, no tracking cookies. We genuinely do not know who you are, and we like it that way.
  • Your data stays yours. Most tools run entirely in your browser. The few that need our server (like the PDF tools) process your file and delete it immediately — we never store, log, or share it.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: Jorres Apps collects nothing about you. No data leaves your browser. No accounts. No tracking. Free forever.

What we collect

Nothing. No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted. There are no analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or third-party data collectors on our pages.

Cookies

We do not use cookies of any kind — not for sessions, not for preferences, not for advertising.

Local storage

  • Some tools save data to your browser's localStorage so your work persists between visits (e.g. Notepad, Kanban Board, Journal).
  • This data stays entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our server or any third party.
  • It can be cleared at any time via your browser settings.

Server logs

Our hosting provider (Render.com) may retain standard HTTP access logs (IP address, timestamp, requested URL) for security and operational purposes. These are not used by us for analytics or marketing.

Bug reports

When you voluntarily submit a bug report, we receive the text you enter (app name, description, and optional email). This is used solely to fix reported issues and is never shared or sold.

Third-party API tools

A few tools (e.g. DNS Lookup, IP Info, Whois Lookup) query public APIs on your behalf. These requests originate from our server and contain no identifying information beyond the query itself.

Children

Jorres Apps is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
