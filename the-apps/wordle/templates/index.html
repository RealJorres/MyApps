WORDLE

How to Play

Guess the WORDLE in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change:

W Green — correct letter, correct spot

I Yellow — letter in word, wrong spot

U Gray — letter not in word

A new word is available each day!
